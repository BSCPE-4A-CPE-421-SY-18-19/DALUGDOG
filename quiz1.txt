Why ASUS PC is not working?

The PC won't boot to BIOS

Possible causes of the problem
1. Faulty RAM
	Solution:
		Try transfering to another slot or replace it.
2. Faulty PCI Cards
3. Misplaced front panel connectors
4. Misplaced power supply cables:
	Solution:
		Check the 24-pin ATX cable and the 4 pin or 8 pin CPU connector.
5. The CPU is dead
6. PSU is not working properly
